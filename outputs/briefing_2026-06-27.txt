### Market Outlook

Silver faces a near-term headwind from rising yields and dollar strength, with institutional forecasts being revised lower. However, the 50%+ decline from recent highs, coupled with technical oversold conditions and the elevated gold/silver ratio, suggests capitulation-phase trading. Recovery likelihood hinges on either (1) reversal of rate-hike expectations, or (2) return to macro drivers supporting precious metals. Intermediate support at $57–$60 is being tested; a breach below $50 would signal deeper correction.
